Research Gap + Problem

Responsible medical AI needs more than high headline accuracy.

Problem

Diabetic foot ulcers are serious complications of diabetes. Early image-based classification can support timely assessment and treatment decisions.

Research Gap

Classical DFU deep learning studies show that CNNs can classify ulcer imagery, but they are mainly classical. Hybrid quantum machine learning evidence for DFU-specific medical imaging remains limited, and ideal quantum results can be misleading.

Project Position

This project connects diabetic foot ulcer classification, transfer learning, hybrid quantum-classical modelling, and multi-metric reliability evaluation.

Perfect separation ≠ reliable prediction

Responsible Claims + Future Work

Strong results, careful boundaries.

Responsible Claims

Research prototype only. Not a clinical diagnostic tool. No clinical readiness claim. No real quantum hardware validation. No quantum advantage claim. No external validation yet.

Future Work

Larger external DFU datasets, patient-level validation, threshold calibration, selected real quantum hardware testing, alternative quantum circuit designs, and severity grading or segmentation.
